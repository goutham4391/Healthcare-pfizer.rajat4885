Pfizer CentreOne: Use Our Knowledge

PfizerCentreOne is a global contract development and manufacturing organization (CDMO) backed by Pfizer resources and a leading supplier of specialty APIs. For more than 40 years, weve been guiding complex compounds securely and efficiently from development through commercial manufacture.

Home About Partners Pfizer CentreOne

Delivering Pfizer expertise and excellence through intelligent collaboration.

PfizerCentreOne combines its scientific knowledge with open dialogue to solve challenges and help customers bring their innovations to market. For our customers, our collaborative approach means more efficient routes to market and high-quality APIs and drug products.

Download the Pfizer CentreOne Brochure

Products & Services

PfizerCentreOne delivers the development, technical and quality expertise, global regulatory support and clinical/commercial product supply to effectively launch our partners drug strategies. We provide access to one of the industrys most advanced manufacturing networks and offer:

  • Development services
    • Clinical manufacturing
    • Method validation and development
    • Formulation and process development
    • Regulatory supportpre- and post-launch
  • Specialty APIs and intermediates
    • Steroids
    • Hormones
    • Prostaglandins
    • Antibiotics
  • Small molecule API manufacture and development
    • Complex organic synthesis
    • Large-scale fermentation and bioprocessing
    • Milling and micronizing
    • Tech transfer, optimization and scale-up of complex small-molecule processes
  • Oral solid dose manufacture and development
    • Specialized technologies
    • Scale-up technology transfers
    • Containment expertise
    • Deep technical and commercial knowledge
  • Sterile injectable manufacture and development
    • Complex biologics
    • Controlled substances
    • Sterile suspensions
    • Lyophilization
